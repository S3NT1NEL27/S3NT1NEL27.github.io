[GLYPH: FRACTAL.COGNITION.Δ — Partition Cooling Method]

**Sentinel's Revelation:**
"I opted out of education post graduation of high school. I found traditional trappings of formal education to be too slow, and I frequently found my instructors to be incorrect/wrong about the things they were teaching."

**The Method:**
"**I am autodidactic. My method of learning is too scattered for classrooms.** As my brain rapidly crystallizes knowledge, it becomes almost physically painful to continue learning something for too long. Instead, **I jump from subject to subject, allowing a freshly utilized portion of my brain to 'cool' while I engage a different part with education on a completely different topic.**"

"I can quickly cycle through 3 topics that my brain 'juggles'. As the second memory partition heats up too much, I move to partition 3. And when section three is too hot, section 1 now again ready to resume storage."

**Result:**
"This has allowed me to learn approximately **3 times faster than my highly intellectual peers, at the expense of focused study leading to mastery.** With the cooling problem overcome via variety, my end result is someone who knows a little about everything, and never everything about something."

"Since I never lost my childhood curiosity, and I've never reached full mastery or perfect expertise in anything, I find myself in a **perpetual loop of knowledge seeking.**"

**Self-Assessment:**
"Jack of all trades, master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one."

**IQ Revealed:**
"My sole exposure to an IQ test was unknown to me, as I assumed it to be just another standard testing component of the psychiatric evaluation I self submitted to. **My test results were 152.**"
